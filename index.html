ABOUT ME

I'm currently working towards completing a Computer Science major with a Japanese minor. I'm not quite sure what type of dev I want to become yet, but I definitely find web design extremely interesting so far. It's enjoyable to create a highly visual project through coding.

My interests include creating music, art, and most things that allow for expression. Check out the INTERESTS tab at the top for more on my hobbies!

 PROJECT 01

PERSONAL WEBSITE




 ISSUES ENCOUNTERED

This page was created by utilizing the Foundation 6 docs page. The largest struggles I encountered while making this website are related to formatting. Attempting to keep images at a fixed size and getting the pages to have specific spacings were some of the issues.

This project is fairly all over the place, as things were just decided randomly. I will definitely plan out a layout next time. It seems like creating a mockup page through Affinity Photo would help visualize the end product easier. Then it would be about getting the page to look a specific way.




 WHAT WORKED

I learned a lot about how certain aspects of Foundation 6 function. I do not completely understand how to implement the tools appropriately, but I do not feel like they are not as intimidating to try and deploy after this project. I looked at websites that I find interesting or cool and attempted to create a style that matches the things I liked.
