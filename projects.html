BANK MANAGEMENT

This application reads in a .csv of transactions and adds those transactions with their dates, transaction type, account type, and amount to accounts with the matching account number. It recognizes LOAN, CHECKING, and SAVINGS accounts. LOAN accounts have a max amount of debt that can be accrued. CHECKING accounts can enter up to -200 dollars in overdraft, but will accrue an overdraft fee. SAVINGS accounts accrue interest.

It then returns the current data that the BANK possesses in the format seen in the image above.

INTERFACED CLOTHING

I have been recently relearning OOP to restrengthen my knowledge base. This project was just to practice creating interfaces and abstract classes to give additional functionality to, in this case, clothing that is also a discountable product.

It lets you assign discounts to clothing, and calculate the tax accordingly.

SHOPPING CART

This program is a classic SHOPPING CART program. It lets you add and remove ITEMS from the SHOPPING CART, calculate the total of the cart, and print out a receipt with pre and post taxation.
